Who are we?

We are one of the largest student-run societies, and the only all-encompassing technology society at University College London. Our membership is open to anyone and everyone in the UCL and IoE community.
We aim to make technology accessible, engaging and fun for our members, whilst encouraging a viral growth of interest in technology in the wider UCL community.
